Site Name - Exploring Curitiba

Preferable domain www.exploringctb.com.br

Site Purpose: The purpose of this website is to help residents and visitors of Curitiba discover a wide range of local attractions, including restaurants, parks, gardens, cultural centers, and community activities. The site will offer categorized listings, interactive tools, and helpful information to support users in exploring their neighborhoods and planning local outings. By organizing and presenting this information in a visually appealing and user-friendly way, the site aims to strengthen community engagement and promote local experiences.

Scenarios:
• “I just moved to Curitiba—how can I find nice parks and outdoor spaces to visit with my family?”
• “I want to explore new restaurants in my neighborhood—what are the top-rated options near me?”
